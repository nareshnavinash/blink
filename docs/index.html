Frequently Asked Questions

What is the 20-20-20 rule?

Every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds. This reduces eye strain from prolonged screen use. Chirp automates this with smart break reminders.

Is Chirp really free?

Yes! Every feature — break reminders, Smart Pause, Pomodoro, custom reminders, stats, team dashboard, and all platforms — is completely free. No premium tier, no upsells, no tracking.

Does Chirp work on all platforms?

Chirp runs natively on macOS, Windows, and Linux desktops, plus iOS and Android mobile. One codebase, same features everywhere.

How does Smart Pause work?

Chirp detects when you're in a meeting (camera/mic active), watching video, in a fullscreen app, or using a focus app — and automatically pauses break reminders until you're done.

Can I use Chirp with my team?

Yes! Chirp includes a team dashboard with centralized settings, aggregate health scores, and admin controls — all free.

Does Chirp collect any data?

No. Chirp has zero analytics, zero telemetry, and zero tracking. Everything stays on your device. Optional sync goes to a server you control — we never see your data.

How is Chirp different from other break reminder apps?

Chirp is free, cross-platform (Mac + Windows + Linux + mobile), and includes Pomodoro, custom reminders, smart pause, and team features. Most alternatives are single-platform, charge $10-30, or lack these features.
